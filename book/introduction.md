# Introduction

**Welcome! What to expect – and why this book is different**

---

Most people who start exploring Bitcoin don't know where to begin. That is the starting point of this book.

Most people who get into Bitcoin make the same first mistake: they leave their Bitcoin on the exchange. It feels safe, because it feels like a bank account. But it is the exact opposite of what Bitcoin was invented for – and it carries real risks you might not yet be aware of.

This book shows you, step by step, how to truly own Bitcoin. How to store it safely and use it in your daily life. No financial jargon, no complexity – just what you really need.

> **DON'T TRUST, VERIFY**  
> The most important principle in the Bitcoin ecosystem: don't trust anyone blindly – not even this book. Verify everything yourself, test each step with small amounts, and only move larger sums once you feel confident.
