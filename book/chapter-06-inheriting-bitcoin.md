# Chapter 6 – Inheriting Bitcoin

**Plan today so your Bitcoin still exists tomorrow**

---

Passing Bitcoin on to your heirs is not a given. Whoever doesn't know the seed has no access – not even rightful heirs. No notary, no court, no lawyer can release Bitcoin when the seed is missing. There is no exception.

> ⚠️ **No Plan, No Inheritance**  
> If you die and no one knows your seed, your Bitcoin is lost forever. Estimates suggest that millions of Bitcoin are already permanently lost – many of them due to missing inheritance planning. The principle of self-custody applies in death as well.

---

## The Fundamental Challenge

You face a dilemma: the more accessible you make your seed for your heirs, the more vulnerable it becomes during your lifetime. The better you hide it, the greater the risk that no one will reach it after your death. This tension cannot be resolved in a single sentence.

| More security while you're alive | More accessibility for heirs |
|---|---|
| Seed known only to you | Heirs know the seed or parts of it |
| Multi-factor authentication required | Easy access when it matters |
| Risk: you die suddenly… | Risk: someone finds it too soon |

---

## Three Basic Principles for Any Method

- Heirs must know that Bitcoin exists – and roughly where to start
- The recovery instructions must be stored separately from the seed
- Every solution should be dry-run tested once before it becomes serious

---

## Simple Methods as a Starting Point

For many beginners, a sealed envelope at a notary or lawyer is a reasonable first step: deposit the seed or a detailed access guide, and reference it in your will. Legally clear, easy to implement.

A written guide for heirs complements this well: where is what? Which software is needed? How does recovery work? Stored separately, but findable when the time comes.

If you want to go deeper, you can look into multisig wallets: multiple keys, of which not all need to be present – e.g. 2 of 3. No single person can act alone, yet access in the event of inheritance is still ensured. Tools: Sparrow Wallet, Nunchuk.

> 💡 **A Test Run Is Mandatory**  
> Show a trusted person the recovery process – with a test seed, not the real one. That's the only way to know everything works when it really matters.
