# The Bitcoin Guide: Get In and Opt Out

**Your practical guide to acquiring and using Bitcoin**

by Michael Wolf | [Bitcoinlighthouse](https://bitcoinlighthouse.de) | Second Edition 2026

🌍 **Languages:** [Deutsch](../README.md) · [English](README.md) · [Español](../libro/README.md)

---

This book explains, step by step, how you can truly own Bitcoin – stored safely, usable in daily life, without financial jargon.

> This is the English translation of the German original [*Der Bitcoin-Ratgeber*](../README.md). The German version is the source of truth; this translation is kept in sync but the German edition may occasionally be ahead.
