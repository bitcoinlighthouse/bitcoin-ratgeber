# Chapter 8 – Connect With the Community

**The Bitcoin community is your strongest resource**

---

Bitcoin is not a passive investment you buy and forget. It's a technology and a movement carried by people. The community is one of the most helpful on the internet – ready to answer questions, help out, and welcome newcomers.

---

## Where to Find the Community

- **Meetups:** einundzwanzig.space/meetups – local meetups in Germany, Austria, Switzerland
- **bitcoin-treff.de** – German-speaking forums and events
- **Telegram:** t.me/bitcoinlighthouse – our newsfeed
- **Nostr** – decentralized, censorship-resistant social media: apps Damus (iOS) or Amethyst (Android)

---

## Podcasts

- **Einundzwanzig** (einundzwanzig.space) – in German, highly recommended for beginners
- **Was Bitcoin bringt** – regular Bitcoin news in German
- **What Bitcoin Did** – well-established English-language Bitcoin podcast

---

## Books for Further Reading

- *The Bitcoin Standard* – Saifedean Ammous
- *21 Lessons* – Gigi (free at 21lessons.com)
- *Gradually then Suddenly* – Parker Lewis
- *The Bullish Case for Bitcoin* – Vijay Boyapati
- *Bitcoin: Everything Divided by 21 Million* – Knut Svanholm
